Job Portal Features

All the tools you need to connect talent with opportunity

Features for Job Seekers

  • User registration and login
  • Profile management and resume upload
  • Job search with filters (location, category, salary, etc.)
  • Apply online and track applications
  • Job alerts and notifications
  • Saved jobs and favorites

Features for Employers

  • Employer registration and login
  • Post, edit, and manage jobs
  • View and manage applicants
  • Search for candidates by skills and experience
  • Employer dashboard with analytics

Features for Admin

  • Manage users and employers
  • Approve or remove job postings
  • Manage categories, skills, and tags
  • Analytics and reports for site activity
  • Content management (pages, banners, blog)

Common & Advanced Features

  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Secure login and data encryption
  • Search engine optimization (SEO)
  • Newsletter and blog integration
  • Optional: AI-based job recommendations
  • Optional: Resume builder, chat system, payment integration
